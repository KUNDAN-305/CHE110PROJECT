  • Reduces pollution in oceans and landfills

  • Lowers greenhouse gas emissions

Products Made from Recycled Plastic

Clothing & Fabrics

Plastic bottles are recycled into polyester fabrics used in clothing, bags, and shoes.

Outdoor Furniture

Recycled plastic is used to create durable outdoor furniture, reducing the need for new plastic.

Packaging Materials

Many companies now use recycled plastic for packaging, cutting down on single-use plastics.

Eco-Friendly Toys

Some toys are now made from recycled plastic, offering safe and sustainable play options.

Innovative Solutions

Recycling technology continues to advance, allowing more types of plastic to be reused in innovative ways. For example, construction materials, road surfaces, and even art installations are being created using recycled plastic.
